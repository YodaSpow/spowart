Email: Video

TOP ^

Strategy:
For the Curry's new brand campaign I was required to integrate a video into the weekly promotional email to support our social media channels and online.

This video runs in devices which support the HTML5 <video> tag (mp4 ogg & webm), falling back to a poster frame then an animated GIF.

Testing: Extensive testing was done across Litmus Builder previews and devices for both Android and iOS (Apple devices). iOS7 supported video tag, however iOS8 does not, therefore a static poster image had to be implemented along with a GIF video clip where the video tag was not supported.

Email: Supplier Funded

TOP ^

Strategy:
Supplier-funded email campaigns generally follow their own creative style which normally does not comply with the master template. (See Template Section below.)
As the master template evolves each week, due to the nature of the ever-changing code requirements to fix glitches
and issues, the master template is used very loosely, more as a guideline to achieve the optimal build, when following the supplier-funded visual layout.

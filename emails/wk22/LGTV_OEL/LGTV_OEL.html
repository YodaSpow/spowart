Discover the world's first curved OLED TV
The brilliance of OLED makes previously impossible performance a reality. The ability to switch off individual pixels renders the blacks truly black, creating an “infinite” contrast ratio. It's exquisite colours also give a more natural, lifelike reproduction of all content. OLED provides super-clear action and it can all be appreciated from a virtually unlimited viewing angle, enhanced by the immersive viewing experience that the curved screen provides.
Stunning picture quality
4 COLOUR PIXEL
LG's advanced 4 Colour Pixel technology adds an unfiltered, white sub-pixel to the traditional red, green and blue. The result? A brighter picture with a wider range of colours and superior colours accuracy.
“INFINITE” CONTRAST
As the first display technology that allows self-lighting pixels to switch on and off individually, OLED offers the first “Infinite” contrast ratio.
MOTION CLARITY
Allowing you to enjoy fast moving scenes with completely blur-free and crystal-clear images.
